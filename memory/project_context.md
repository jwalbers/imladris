**IMLADRIS** = IMaging Lab for Digital Radiography Information Systems — a virtual integration lab for preparing a clinical deployment.

**Name reference:** Imladris is Rivendell in Sindarin (Tolkien) — a hidden valley, place of healing and refuge. Intentional double meaning for a medical imaging lab.

**Target hospital:** Botsabelo Hospital, Lesotho. A former leper colony; "Botsabelo" means "place of refuge" in Sesotho. Now focused on MDR-TB care — the parallel with Imladris (place of refuge/healing) is intentional and meaningful.

**Why:** The lab simulates the full clinical imaging workflow (DICOM modality → cloud PACS → radiology workstation → clinician) using a fictional MDR-TB patient population, so staff can train before the real system goes live.

**How to apply:** Keep this context in mind when naming things, writing documentation, or making design decisions — the mission is real-world clinical deployment in a resource-limited setting.
